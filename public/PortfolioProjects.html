Overwatch Competetive Game Tracker (Current)

When Overwatch 2 released in late 2022, the competetive mode required you to win 5 games, or lose 15 before displaying your new rank. Between ranked displays there was no way for you to see how many you had won or lost. I designed a spreadsheet so I could track this.

Since then the development team have reworked the skill rating system so it is similar to Overwatch 1, however I still want to develop this game tracker. I feel like it could be a useful tool for coaching and VOD review, and also could help to fill the gap in analysis that cannot be done in the game.
